Audience

Our audience will be family groups and school (elementary, secondary or college groups). We look for guests with an appetite for experiences to remember as groups of friends or families. Our common customer looks for a place to have memories to share, experience the nature and have some desire to try new things. Our customers, favor the location, and facilities we offer, and will appreciate the secure measures and seriousness of a company with long experience in the field. We will focus in offering to family and educational institutions, economical attractive packages in order to consolidate a tradition to return to a place where they feel belong to.
